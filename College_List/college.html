Annual IIM Ahmedabad MBA Fees: The total programme fee for PGP courses offered at IIM Ahmedabad ranges between INR 12 lakh to INR 25 lakh, with IIM Ahmedabad being the most expensive among all. At present, the total programme fee payable for the MBA course is approximately INR 25 lakh. The amount is inclusive of: • INR 18,19,000 Tuition fee + GST • Laptops, boarding, laundry, clothes, and personal expenses are extra.

IIM Ahmedabad Fee Structure for MBA (2024-26 batch)


IIM Ahmedabad Application Fees 2,400 IIM Ahmedabad Tuition Fees 18,19,000 IIM Ahmedabad Other Expenses 25,00,000 (approx) • Note: The registration fees are to be paid after the offer of admission

Detailed Eligibility Criteria:

• Academic Qualification:


• A bachelor's degree with at least 50% marks or equivalent CGPA (45% for SC/ST/PwD).
• The bachelor's degree must be from a recognized university or institution.
• The degree must entail a minimum of three years of education after completing higher secondary schooling (10+2) or equivalent.
• For candidates awarded grades/CGPA, the equivalence should be certified by their institution.
• CAT Score:
• A valid CAT score is required for shortlisting.
• The cut-off percentile varies based on the category (General, NC-OBC, SC, ST, etc.).
• Top IIMs, including IIM Ahmedabad, often have cut-off percentages of 99+ for the General category.
• AWT and PI:
• Successful completion of the Academic Writing Test (AWT) and Personal Interview (PI) is mandatory.
• The AWT and PI rounds carry significant weightage in the final selection process.

IIM Bangalore Fees: The Indian Institute of Management Bangalore (IIMB) is one of the premier institutes offering management studies in India. IIMB has been hailed as an Institute of National Importance under the 2017 IIM Act. IIM Bangalore has laid a history of hiking the programme fee for its flagship MBA programmes in past years. Shiksha brings you a breakdown of the fee structure for MBA at IIM Bangalore and the scholarships offered.

IIM Bangalore Fee Structure for MBA (2024-2026 Batch): Particulars Fee (in INR) IIM Bangalore MBA fee (2 years)* 26,00,000 Caution deposit (General) 15,000 Caution deposit (Hostel) 5,000 Mess Advance 30,000 (each semester)

Detailed Eligibility Criteria:

• Education: A bachelor's degree in any discipline with a minimum of 50% marks or equivalent CGPA is required. • For SC, ST, and PwD categories: A minimum of 45% or equivalent CGPA is required. • Work Experience: Work experience requirements differ for each program. For example, the PGPEM program requires a minimum of 4 years of full-time work experience after obtaining a bachelor's degree. • Entrance Exams: A valid CAT, GMAT, GRE, or IIMB Test score is required. The test score must be valid as on the date of application submission. • Specific Program Requirements: IIM Bangalore offers various programs, including the MBA, MBA in Business Analytics, PGPEM, and PGPPM, each with specific eligibility and selection criteria.
